❓ Frequently Asked Questions
🔒 Is my PDF secure? Does it get uploaded to a server?
Absolutely not. All conversion happens 100% in your browser using PDF.js. Your PDF file never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server.
📐 What DPI should I choose?
For screen use: 72–144 DPI. For printing: 288–360 DPI. For maximum quality: 432–576 DPI. Higher DPI = larger file size and longer conversion time.
📦 How many pages can I convert at once?
There is no hard limit. You can convert PDFs with hundreds of pages. Performance depends on your device's CPU and RAM. Large PDFs at high DPI may take a few minutes.
💾 Can I download all pages at once?
Yes! Click "Download All (ZIP)" to get all converted PNG images packed into a single ZIP archive. You can also select specific pages and download just those.
