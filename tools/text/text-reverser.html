FAQ

What does "Reverse Characters" do? It reverses every character in your text. "Hello" becomes "olleH".
What is Mirror Text? Each letter is replaced by a visually mirrored Unicode character.
Is my text stored anywhere? No! Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.
What is a palindrome? A word/phrase that reads the same forwards and backwards, like "racecar".
