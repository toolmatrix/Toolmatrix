Introduction

Traditional image upscaling stretches pixels, creating blurry results. AI upscaling uses deep learning to intelligently add detail — sharpening edges, enhancing textures and filling in missing information. The result looks like it was shot at higher resolution.

How AI Upscaling Works

AI upscalers (based on models like Real-ESRGAN and ESRGAN) analyze millions of high-resolution images to learn what high-quality details look like. When upscaling your image, they predict and add the missing pixels rather than just stretching existing ones.

When to Use AI Upscaling

  • Print a small digital photo at large size
  • Restore old, low-resolution photos
  • Upscale product images for e-commerce zoom
  • Enhance screenshots for presentations
  • Improve thumbnail quality for YouTube
  • Upscale AI-generated images for printing

How to Upscale an Image

1

Upload Image

Upload any JPG, PNG, or WebP. Works best on images under 2000px wide.

Select 2x (double size) or 4x (quadruple). 4x is best for very small or pixelated images.
