How to Use the Age Calculator

Our free online age calculator helps you find your exact age from your date of birth. Whether you need to know your age for official documents, forms, or just out of curiosity, this tool gives you precise results instantly.

How Does the Age Calculator Work?

Simply enter your date of birth and optionally select a specific date to calculate your age at. Click the "Calculate My Age" button and get detailed results including:

  • Exact age in years, months, and days
  • Total months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes you've lived
  • Next birthday countdown - days remaining until your birthday
  • Zodiac sign based on your birth date
  • Day of the week you were born on
  • Live age counter updating every second

Why Use Our Age Calculator?

Our age calculator is the most accurate and feature-rich tool available online. It accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and provides real-time age tracking. It's perfect for:

  • Calculating age for passport applications
  • Finding age for school admissions
  • Checking retirement eligibility
  • Calculating age difference between two dates
  • Fun facts about how many days you've been alive

Age Calculation Formula

The age is calculated by finding the difference between the current date (or selected date) and your birth date. The calculation considers the exact number of days in each month and accounts for leap years to give you the most accurate result possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate my exact age?
To calculate your exact age, enter your date of birth in the age calculator above and click "Calculate My Age". The tool will instantly show your age in years, months, days, and even hours, minutes, and seconds.
How many days have I been alive?
Our age calculator shows you the total number of days you've been alive. Simply enter your birth date and check the "Total Days" section in the results. It accounts for all leap years in the calculation.
Can I calculate age between two specific dates?
Yes! Use the "Age at Date" field to select any date. The calculator will find the exact age/difference between your birth date and the selected date. If left empty, it uses today's date.
Does the calculator account for leap years?
Absolutely! Our age calculator takes leap years into account for 100% accurate results. The total days calculation includes all leap days between your birth date and the current date.
What is my zodiac sign?
After calculating your age, the tool automatically shows your zodiac sign based on your birth date. There are 12 zodiac signs including Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces.
Is my data safe with this age calculator?
Yes, 100% safe! All calculations happen directly in your browser. We don't store, collect, or send your data anywhere. Your privacy is completely protected.
